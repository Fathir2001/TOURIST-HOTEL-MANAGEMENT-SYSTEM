Common Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Breakfast inclusion depends on the room package you book. Some packages include complimentary breakfast while others offer it as an add-on. Please check your booking details or contact us for specific information.

Do you offer airport transportation?

Yes, we provide airport pickup and drop-off services at an additional cost. Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance to arrange the transportation. You can request this service during booking or by contacting our front desk.

Is Wi-Fi available at the hotel?

Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms, lobby, restaurant, and common areas.

Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site parking for all our guests. Valet parking service is also available upon request.

Are pets allowed at the hotel?

We have a pet-friendly policy for small pets (under 10kg). Please inform us in advance if you're traveling with a pet. Additional charges and terms apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and cash payments. Online bookings can be made using credit/debit cards or digital payment platforms.

Do you have facilities for disabled guests?

Yes, we have wheelchair-accessible rooms and facilities throughout the hotel. Please inform us of any special requirements when making your reservation.

Hotel Policies

Cancellation Policy

  • Free Cancellation: Up to 48 hours before check-in
  • Late Cancellation: 24-48 hours - 50% charge
  • No-Show: Full charge of first night
  • Special Events: Non-refundable during peak seasons

Check-In / Check-Out Times

  • Check-In: 2:00 PM onwards
  • Check-Out: 11:00 AM
  • Early Check-In: Subject to availability (additional charge may apply)
  • Late Check-Out: Available until 2:00 PM (50% of room rate)

Children Policy

  • Under 6 years: Free of charge
  • 6-12 years: 50% of adult rate
  • Above 12 years: Charged as adult
  • Extra Bed: Available on request (additional charge)

Other Policies

  • Non-Smoking: All rooms are non-smoking
  • ID Proof: Required at check-in
  • Damage: Guests liable for any damage
  • Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM
