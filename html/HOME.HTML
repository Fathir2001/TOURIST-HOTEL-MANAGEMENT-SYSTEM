Welcome to Bandarawela Beautiful Tourist Hotel

Escape to Sri Lanka's hill country and immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Bandarawela. Imagine vast, emerald tea plantations stretching across majestic mountains. Breathe in the crisp, mountain air and feel the cool mist kiss your skin. Bandarawela, a haven of tranquility, beckons you to rejuvenate your soul amidst stunning scenery.

Make your Bandarawela escape unforgettable with a stay at the historic Bandarawela Hotel. Steeped in colonial charm, this classic hotel offers a unique opportunity to experience a bygone era. Indulge in the region's bounty with fresh strawberries, pears, and of course, the finest Ceylon tea.

Unveil a Century of Comfort at Bandarawela Hotels. Immerse yourself in the timeless charm of Bandarawela's historic hotels. Step back in time and experience the warmth of rooms that have endured for over a century. These abodes, though whispering tales of the past, still offer a touch of modern comfort to ensure a truly unforgettable stay.
