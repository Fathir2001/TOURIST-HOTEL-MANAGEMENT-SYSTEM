THINGS TO DO

THINGS TO DO BANDARAWELA

  • Things to do Bandarawela
  • Adventure, Culture and Exploration in the Mountains

    • The best things to do in the Ella region include scenic treks to Little Adam’s Peak, Ella Rock and Lipton’s Seat, visits to ancient temples or plunges into hidden mountain waterfalls such as Diyaluma waterfall. Return to The Orient Hotel and experience a coconut arrack tasting or rejuvenate in our Ayurvedic Spa. As the leading adventure hotel in the region and we are proud to be able to offer all of the below curated excursions and experiences to our guests. Our in-house guides are local experts and will ensure you have the perfect time in the Ella region.

  • EXCURSIONS

  • Horton Plains Nature

    A UNESCO World Heritage site, famed for its rich diversity of plants and animals, this challenging Trek is a nature lovers dream while also incorporating incredible views at World`s

    Hike to Lipton`s seat

    Hike to Lipton`s seat Tea with a View Trek through leafy green tea estates to Lipton`s seat, famous for its stunning view of the Ella region`s misty mountains. Our Trek also includes a stop at a local Hindu Temple.

    Trek to Diyaluma Waterfall

    Trek to Diyaluma Waterfall An Unforgetable Thing to do in Ella Upper Diyaluma is a majestic waterfall with incredible mountain views. More off the beaten path, Our Trek includes stops at several scenic natural pools.

    Hike to Ella Rock - Trek to Ella Rock

    Hike to Ella Rock Trek to Ella Rock The hike up Ella Rock is a great way to explore and sight see some of the scenic attractions in the area. Just a short drive away from Orient Hotel, this trek includes transportation along with a picnic meal

    The Temple Stay Experience

    The Temple Stay Experience Immerse Yourself in an Ancient Culture Visit monks at an ancient Ella Temple. Share a traditional meal with them, followed by a sermon and meditation lesson. Spend the night at the temple, to get an immersive experience into a simple way of life,

  • The Pekoe Trail Stage 15A Stunning Trek Including Ella Rock

  • One of the most popular stages in the world famous Pekoe Trail, this scenic trek incorporates key sights including the summit of Ella Rock and stops at Ella Town.

  • Pekoe Trail Stage 16 Bridges, Railways and Tea Fields

    • A wonderful trek starting in Ella town and ending at Demodara via scenic tea estates and the instagram favourite Nine Arches Bridge

  • EXPERIENCE
  • Coconut Arrack Tasting Try the National Drink

    • A perfect evening activity for visitors to the Ella region, experience the signature taste of Sri Lanka in a guided tasting provided by our beverages expert.

  • Cocktail Mixology and Tasting Become a Cocktail Expert

    • Our beverage experts will teach you the secrets of making the perfect cocktail. Taste a broad range of cocktails, from classic favourites to unique local mixes

  • Tour Our Organic Farm A Gardener's Paradise

    • Explore our on site organic farm with a free guided tour

  • Ayurvedic Spa Treatments Rejuvenate in the mountains

    • Experience the healing power of the ancient art of Ayurveda at our Spa in Bandarawela. Our therapists will use ancient herbal oil blends and massage techniques to relax and rejuvenate your body. Ideal after a long trek.

  • Traditional Sri Lankan Cooking Lessons Become a Master Chef

    • During this Family Friendly activity, our expert chefs will teach you the secrets of traditional Sri Lankan recipes. Improve your cooking skills and surprise your friends when they next visit you with new exotic recipes.

  • Dining in our Tranquil Garden A Unique Dining Experience

    • A romantic evening activity in Bandarawela, enjoy a delicious meal while gazing at beautiful mountain views.
