Things to Do in Bandarawela

Adventure, Culture and Exploration in the Mountains

The best things to do in the Ella region include scenic treks to Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock and Lipton's Seat, visits to ancient temples or plunges into hidden mountain waterfalls such as Diyaluma waterfall. Return to The Orient Hotel and experience a coconut arrack tasting or rejuvenate in our Ayurvedic Spa. As the leading adventure hotel in the region, we are proud to be able to offer all of the below curated excursions and experiences to our guests. Our in-house guides are local experts and will ensure you have the perfect time in the Ella region.

Excursions

Horton Plains Nature Trek

UNESCO World Heritage Site

A UNESCO World Heritage site, famed for its rich diversity of plants and animals, this challenging trek is a nature lover's dream while also incorporating incredible views at World's End.

Full Day Challenging

Hike to Lipton's Seat

Tea with a View

Trek through leafy green tea estates to Lipton's seat, famous for its stunning view of the Ella region's misty mountains. Our trek also includes a stop at a local Hindu Temple.

Half Day Moderate

Trek to Diyaluma Waterfall

An Unforgettable Experience

Upper Diyaluma is a majestic waterfall with incredible mountain views. More off the beaten path, our trek includes stops at several scenic natural pools.

Full Day Moderate

Hike to Ella Rock

Scenic Mountain Trek

The hike up Ella Rock is a great way to explore and sight see some of the scenic attractions in the area. Just a short drive away from Orient Hotel, this trek includes transportation along with a picnic meal.

Half Day Moderate

The Temple Stay Experience

Immerse Yourself in Ancient Culture

Visit monks at an ancient Ella Temple. Share a traditional meal with them, followed by a sermon and meditation lesson. Spend the night at the temple to get an immersive experience into a simple way of life.

Overnight Easy

The Pekoe Trail Stage 15A

Stunning Trek Including Ella Rock

One of the most popular stages in the world famous Pekoe Trail, this scenic trek incorporates key sights including the summit of Ella Rock and stops at Ella Town.

Full Day Challenging

Pekoe Trail Stage 16

Bridges, Railways and Tea Fields

A wonderful trek starting in Ella town and ending at Demodara via scenic tea estates and the Instagram favourite Nine Arches Bridge.

Full Day Moderate

Experiences

Try the National Drink

A perfect evening activity for visitors to the Ella region, experience the signature taste of Sri Lanka in a guided tasting provided by our beverages expert.

1 Hour Adults Only

Become a Cocktail Expert

Our beverage experts will teach you the secrets of making the perfect cocktail. Taste a broad range of cocktails, from classic favourites to unique local mixes.

Explore our on-site organic farm with a free guided tour. Learn about sustainable farming practices and see where our fresh ingredients come from.

1 Hour Family Friendly

Ayurvedic Spa Treatments

Rejuvenate in the Mountains

Experience the healing power of the ancient art of Ayurveda at our Spa in Bandarawela. Our therapists will use ancient herbal oil blends and massage techniques to relax and rejuvenate your body. Ideal after a long trek.

1-2 Hours All Ages

Traditional Sri Lankan Cooking

Become a Master Chef

During this family-friendly activity, our expert chefs will teach you the secrets of traditional Sri Lankan recipes. Improve your cooking skills and surprise your friends when they next visit you with new exotic recipes.

3 Hours Family Friendly

Dining in our Tranquil Garden

A Unique Dining Experience

A romantic evening activity in Bandarawela, enjoy a delicious meal while gazing at beautiful mountain views in our serene garden setting.

2 Hours Romantic
