This Property

Comfortable Accommodations: Bandarawela Hotel in Bandarawela offers family rooms with private bathrooms, garden views, and modern amenities. Each room includes air-conditioning, a work desk, and free WiFi.

Dining and Leisure: Guests can enjoy a family-friendly restaurant serving Chinese, British, French, Indian, and local cuisines. The bar offers cocktails and high tea. The hotel features a sun terrace, outdoor fireplace, and free bicycles.

Convenient Location: Located 59 mi from Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport, the hotel is a 6-minute walk from Bandarawela Railway Station. Nearby attractions include Gregory Lake (27 mi) and Demodara Nine Arch Bridge (8.7 mi).
